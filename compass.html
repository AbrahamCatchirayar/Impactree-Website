Compass Series

A 360° Sustainability Intelligence Platform
In depth performance tracking and insights across 15 ESG pillars

The Compass Series offers intuitive tools to help businesses set ESG baselines, define goals, and track progress in areas like carbon, energy, and social impact. It turns complex data into simple, actionable insights—making sustainability a clear, strategic advantage.

Compass includes six purpose-built tools aligned to Impactree’s 15-pillar ESG framework. It gives organizations a 360° view of their sustainability performance across carbon, energy, vendors, and social metrics.

Perfect for companies starting their sustainability journey or looking to turn ESG into long-term value. Whether in manufacturing, logistics, or services—Compass delivers clarity, control, and confidence.

Track your progress, identify areas for improvement, and demonstrate your commitment to sustainability with verifiable data. Compass empowers you to make informed decisions and drive meaningful change.

Energy Management

Optimize Your Energy Footprint

Gain complete visibility and control over your energy consumption. Our Energy Management module allows you to track, analyze, and optimize energy usage across your operations, identifying opportunities for significant savings and reduced environmental impact.

  • Real-time energy monitoring
  • Usage pattern analysis and anomaly detection
  • Goal setting and progress tracking
  • Customizable reporting for compliance and insights

Performance Management

Drive Continuous Improvement

Measure and enhance your overall ESG performance with our comprehensive Performance Management tool. Set key performance indicators (KPIs), monitor progress against targets, and identify areas for strategic improvements to bolster your sustainability profile.

  • Centralized ESG data hub
  • KPI tracking and dashboards
  • Automated data collection and validation
  • Performance benchmarking against industry peers

Carbon Management

Achieve Your Net-Zero Goals

Effectively measure, report, and reduce your carbon footprint. Our Carbon Management module provides granular insights into Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions, helping you develop and implement robust decarbonization strategies.

  • Automated emissions calculation
  • Scenario planning for reduction targets
  • Compliance reporting (e.g., GHG Protocol)
  • Carbon offset and credit tracking

Vendor Management

Build a Sustainable Supply Chain

Assess and manage the ESG performance of your vendors and suppliers. Our Vendor Management module helps you ensure your supply chain aligns with your sustainability goals, mitigating risks and fostering responsible sourcing.

  • Supplier ESG risk assessment
  • Performance monitoring and scorecards
  • Collaborative improvement initiatives
  • Supply chain transparency and traceability

Safety Management

Ensure a Safe and Healthy Workplace

Promote a culture of safety and ensure compliance with our Safety Management module. Track incidents, manage risks, and implement preventative measures to protect your workforce and minimize operational disruptions.

  • Incident reporting and analysis
  • Risk assessment and mitigation planning
  • Safety training management
  • Compliance with health and safety regulations

Social Impact Management

Maximize Your Positive Societal Influence

Measure and enhance your positive impact on communities and stakeholders. Our Social Impact Management module helps you track initiatives related to diversity, community engagement, employee well-being, and ethical practices.

  • Community engagement tracking
  • Diversity and inclusion metrics
  • Employee well-being programs monitoring
  • Ethical supply chain practices
