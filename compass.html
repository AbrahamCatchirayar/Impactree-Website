Compass Series

A 360° Sustainability Intelligence Platform
In depth performance tracking and insights across 15 ESG pillars

The Compass Series is a set of easy-to-use tools that help businesses understand and improve their sustainability and ESG efforts. It covers everything from carbon emissions and energy use to social impact and ethical sourcing. Designed for companies starting their ESG journey, it simplifies complex data into clear, actionable insights. Compass turns sustainability into a measurable and strategic advantage.

The Compass Series is a suite of 6 purpose-built tools that guide businesses through their Sustainability and Performance Management journey. Each product is mapped to Impactree's proprietary 15-pillar ESG framework, providing a 360° view of your organization’s Environmental, Social, and Governance efforts—from carbon emissions and energy use to vendor ethics and social impact.
Ideal for companies that are in the early stages of their sustainability journey, or those beginning to recognize ESG as a lever for long-term value. Whether you're a manufacturer navigating energy efficiency, a logistics firm mapping carbon footprints, or a service provider seeking to enhance social performance—Compass Series products offer clarity, control, and confidence.
The Compass Series equips organizations with simple yet powerful tools to demystify ESG and sustainability. Instead of scattered reports and ad-hoc assessments, Compass offers structured insights that lead to measurable outcomes.
Whether you're in manufacturing, real estate, logistics, consumer goods, finance, or technology, these tools help you:
Key Benefits
  • Cut energy and operational costs through data-backed efficiency.
  • Accelerate your carbon reduction journey with transparent tracking.
  • Build ethical, responsible supply chains that reduce compliance risks.
  • Ensure workplace safety while fostering a zero-harm culture.
  • Measure and communicate the real ROI of your sustainability programs.
  • Showcase your social impact with data-rich storytelling.
In a world where stakeholders—from investors to customers—demand accountability, Compass makes ESG tangible, actionable, and aligned with your business goals.
