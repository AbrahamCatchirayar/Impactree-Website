The Navigator Series

Three platforms, One purpose: Strategic Resilience
Data-driven insights for future-proof decisions.

The Navigator Series provides advanced tools for businesses to leverage ESG data for strategic decisions. It offers customized solutions for unique sustainability needs, combining software with expert consulting to drive business value beyond compliance. Ideal for companies with sustainable systems, it identifies risks, spots opportunities, and enhances performance, turning insights into a competitive edge.

Navigator is a premium suite of three expert-driven solutions that combine data intelligence with consultingdesigned to integrate ESG into investment and business strategy.
Built for businesses and funds with established ESG systems, Navigator helps expand impact, enhance performance, and future-proof decisionsideal for strategists, fund managers, and sustainability leads.
As ESG maturity grows, so do the expectationsfrom regulators, shareholders, and customers. The Navigator Series helps organizations make strategic decisions rooted in ESG intelligence, not intuition.
For private equity, asset managers, corporates, and infrastructure firms, Navigator helps identify high-impact ESG strategies, benchmark performance, build resilient investment plans, and deliver clear, actionable insights for leadership.
Key Benefits
  • Identifying high-ROI sustainability strategies
  • Benchmarking performance across departments or portfolios
  • Building risk-resilient investment theses
  • Empowering leadership with clear, contextual ESG insights
