Scope 3 Management

A centralized, intelligent feature designed to help auto ancillary manufacturers measure, manage, and mitigate Scope 3 emissions across their entire supply chain. This feature combines supplier data integration, real-time analytics, and collaboration tools to bring complete transparency and control to emissions that traditionally lie outside your direct operations.

By addressing all tiers of your supply chain—including raw material providers, component suppliers, logistics partners, and assembly vendors—we empower organizations to align with sustainability goals while remaining compliant and competitive in a fast-evolving regulatory environment.

Regulatory Compliance

Real-time regulatory radar. It provides localized, up-to-date insights into emissions regulations across regions—helping auto ancillary businesses stay compliant without constantly chasing shifting rules.

With automated alerts, built-in policy trackers, and integration with global compliance frameworks (like EU’s CSRD or India’s BRSR), we ensure you're always audit-ready and aligned with the latest legal expectations. It simplifies the complexity of reporting and reduces the risk of non-compliance.

Custom Dashboards

We deliver dynamic, site-level and equipment-specific emissions dashboards tailored for auto ancillary manufacturers. This feature helps you visualize emissions across factories, lines, or individual machines—enabling data-driven reduction strategies and precise targeting of high-emission zones.

Whether you’re looking to benchmark facilities, optimize equipment performance, or prepare ESG reports, Impactree makes complex emissions data actionable and accessible for all stakeholders.

Supplier collaboration

Bridges the transparency gap in your supply chain by enabling seamless engagement with suppliers on sustainability goals. Through shared dashboards, ESG performance tracking, and structured data requests, it ensures consistent and reliable sustainability reporting from all upstream partners.

The platform also supports onboarding new suppliers with ESG scorecards, automates check-ins, and fosters continuous improvement through progress tracking and incentives for greener practices.
